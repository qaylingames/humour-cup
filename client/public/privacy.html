Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to Humour Cup. This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

Humour Cup is a multiplayer party game. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide sensitive personal information to play. The names and text inputs (scenarios and jokes) you provide during gameplay are temporarily processed to facilitate the game room and are not permanently stored or tied to your personal identity.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • We utilize a Consent Management Platform (CMP) to gather necessary user consent regarding tracking and personalized ads, specifically for users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK.

3. Log Data and Analytics

Like many websites, our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect basic log data that your browser sends whenever you visit. This data may include information such as your computer's Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other diagnostic data. This is used strictly to maintain server health and performance.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
